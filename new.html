HOW THIS WORKS

Each story has five core Ideas in it. Each Idea should have 3 to 5 words. In the box (using your voice or keyboard - see below), enter an idea and press "Next". Then enter your second idea, and press Next. Repeat this for each of the 5 ideas.

KEYBOARD

Tap in the "Ideas go here..." box, and start typing in your idea using the keyboard that pops up.

VOICE

Tap in the "Ideas go here..." box. On the keyboard that pops up, look for a button with the icon. Tap it, and start speaking.



Tap the help button to dismiss.
