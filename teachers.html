Quick Help

Write My Idea! is a platform for students to write their stories, without being restrained by an inability to write. It supports voice input, and allows kids to express their ideas in a simple way.
Each story comprises of 5 "core ideas". Each idea is one part of the story (for example: "I went to the beach." is one idea).

Frequently Asked Questions

The icon isn't showing up on the keyboard.

The microphone icon might not show up if you haven't enabled Dictation on your device. Enter your device's settings app, and search for "dictation" or "speech" and make sure it's enabled.

Can students put in less than 5 ideas?

Not yet. Currently, the requirement is 5, but this may change later.

Why is the ideas box red?

The idea box turns red when a bad word has been entered. Tap to clear the box, and try again.

How do I print the worksheets?

On iOS: tap the Share icon (), scroll across on the third row, and tap Print.
On Android: look for a Share button/icon and tap it. Find the Print option, and tap it.
On Mac: press ⌘+P.
On Windows/Linux: press Ctrl+P.

Why isn't Dictation recognising my words?

Your dictation services might not pick up the words you say properly for one of two reasons:

  1. New device - If you're using a new device, the speech recognition service on it may not have adjusted to your particular voice yet.
  2. Speech settings - Check your speech settings. It may be that your device is set to be trained to a certain accent or language. Change it to suit you and try again.
